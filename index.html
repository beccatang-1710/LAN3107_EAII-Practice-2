Workshop Planning Meeting

Introduction — Opening the Meeting
Item 1
▾
B: Good morning, everyone. Welcome to today's meeting. Shall we start now?
All: Yes.
📝
Agenda Setting
Item 1
▾
C: Does everyone have a copy of the agenda?
All: Yes.
D: Great! The purpose of today's meeting is to discuss the arrangements for our upcoming workshop, which aims to educate the public about green living.
A: We are going to cover three agenda items: promotional strategies for the workshop; green living tips to cover in the workshop; and any other business that you would like to discuss.
📣
Discussion — Promotional Strategies
Item 2
▾
C: Let's begin by discussing our promotional strategies for the workshop. I suggest that we consider using social media platforms such as Instagram and Facebook. Since they have a wide reach, I believe they can effectively attract younger audiences, who are generally eco-conscious and interested in tips for green living.
B: I agree. This approach would certainly help us engage people in a cost-effective way. In addition, why don't we collaborate with local eco-friendly influencers? This would allow us to reach their followers and add credibility to our message. Do you see my point?
D: That's a fantastic idea. I would also prefer to use influencer partnerships rather than printed posters or flyers, because influencers can create authentic engagement and reach niche eco-conscious communities more effectively.
A: Yes, that's exactly my point of view. By adopting these strategies, we could reach a broader audience. That said, could we also consider distributing digital flyers at community centers and schools to supplement our online efforts?
C: Umm, I'm afraid it might be impractical to rely on digital flyers, as this could create unnecessary digital clutter. Instead, I propose creating short video clips on sustainable living and sharing them on platforms like TikTok and YouTube, which would be more engaging and shareable.
D: I see what you mean. Building on that, I also think we should avoid traditional approaches such as paid newspaper advertisements, as they are unlikely to be effective nowadays, with most people relying on digital platforms for information.
✅
Decision-Making on Promotion
▾
B: OK, so we've heard several suggestions now. It seems that we have a consensus that the workshop should be promoted through social media platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube, along with collaborating with local eco-friendly influencers to enhance credibility and engagement. Can we all agree that we should present these two options to the management team?
All: Sure.
🌿
Discussion — Green Living Tips
Item 3
▾
C: Excellent! Let's move on to the next item on the agenda.
D: What are your opinions on the green living tips we should highlight in the workshop?
A: I'd like to suggest making minimizing energy consumption a priority. Teaching participants to switch to LED lighting and energy-efficient appliances would be very practical and effective in the long run.
B: I agree. Energy saving is important. In addition, we could also encourage people to reduce paper usage by switching to digital alternatives, such as e-bills and e-documents, since this can be implemented immediately at little or no cost.
C: Although paper reduction is easier to implement, reducing energy consumption offers greater long-term benefits. The former is low-cost and quickly achievable, whereas the latter focuses on sustained environmental impact. Therefore, I believe both should be highlighted.
D: In my view, we should encourage the use of public transport, which would further reduce carbon emissions.
All: Wonderful.
B: What if we also add basic water conservation practices, such as fixing leaks and using low-flow fixtures? This could help participants save water and lower their utility bills.
A: I'm afraid I disagree. Some water-saving measures may involve higher costs, which could discourage certain participants.
C: I see your point. Personally, I think affordability should be a key criterion when selecting our tips, so that everyone can take part regardless of their budget.
D: I can't agree with you more. In that case, why don't we combine all four practices—energy saving, paper reduction, public transport, and basic water conservation—while keeping the focus on low-cost and practical actions?
B: It seems that we can all agree that these four practices should be introduced as green living tips in the workshop?
All: Yes.
A: And can we also agree that we should prepare clear examples and supporting materials for each of these practices?
All: Sure.
🎁
Any Other Business & Closing
Item 4
▾
B: Fantastic! Does anyone have any other business they would like to discuss?
D: Yes, I'd like to discuss souvenirs for participants. I suggest giving them recycled tote bags, because they are reusable, practical, and promote sustainability.
B: That's a good idea. I also recommend printing our workshop logo and a green living slogan on the bags to reinforce our message. Do we all agree?
C: Both of your ideas are sensible, but I'd prefer reusable food wraps as souvenirs. They are more practical in daily life, as they can help reduce plastic waste more directly.
A: Although recycled tote bags are useful for shopping, reusable food wraps may be more effective in reducing single-use plastic. While tote bags are durable and visible, food wraps are used more frequently, which makes them more environmentally impactful. Therefore, I think both options have their strengths. Shall we all agree on choosing both as souvenirs?
All: Fantastic.
A: However, instead of choosing one over the other, what about offering participants a choice between recycled tote bags and reusable food wraps, so they can select what suits them best?
D: I can't agree with you more. Giving participants a choice will make the souvenirs more appealing and ensure they are used more regularly.
C: Alright. Let's present this idea to management as well. Does anyone have any other suggestions?
All: Not right now.
A: I think we should end our discussion there. To summarize, we've agreed to focus on social media and influencer partnerships for promotion, cover all four green living practices as tips, and offer a choice of eco-friendly souvenirs.
D: Well, I think that's all we have time for today. We have some really great ideas to take to management. Does anybody have anything else to add?
All: No.
B: Well, if nobody has anything else to add, I think we can finish here. Thank you very much for attending the meeting and making contributions, everybody.

Meeting Outcomes

📣 Promotion Social media (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube) + eco-friendly influencer partnerships
🌿 Green Tips Energy saving · Paper reduction · Public transport · Water conservation
🎁 Souvenirs Participants choose between recycled tote bags or reusable food wraps
📎 Next Steps Prepare examples & supporting materials; present decisions to management team
